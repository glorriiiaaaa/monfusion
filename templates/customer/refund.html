Refund & Return Policy 🔄

Last updated: January 2024

Since all Mons Fusion products are custom-made to order, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties.

Eligible Refunds

  • Quality defects or printing errors on our part
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged product received

Refund Process

  • Raise a request within 48 hours of delivery with photo evidence
  • Approved refunds processed within 5–7 business days
  • Refunds credited to original payment method

Non-Refundable

  • Customer errors in customisation details
  • Change of mind after production starts
  • Delayed delivery due to incorrect address

Contact

For refund requests: returns@monsfusion.com or WhatsApp us within 48 hours.
