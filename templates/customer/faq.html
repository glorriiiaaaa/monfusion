FAQ ❓

Quick answers to common questions

📦 Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?▼
All orders are delivered within 3–5 business days across India. Festival season orders may take slightly longer. We'll keep you updated via email and WhatsApp.
What is the minimum order value?▼
The minimum order value is ₹150. A delivery charge of ₹80 applies. Orders above ₹499 get free delivery!
How do I track my order?▼
Track your order from the 'My Orders' section in your profile. We also send tracking updates via WhatsApp once your order ships.
Do you ship outside India?▼
Currently we only ship within India. We're working on expanding internationally — follow @monsfusion for updates!

🎨 Customisation

How do I share photos for customisation?▼
After placing your order, our team will reach out via WhatsApp or email to collect your photos and personalisation details.
Can I preview my design before printing?▼
Yes! We send a digital preview for all custom orders before going to print. You can request changes before final production.
What photo quality is needed?▼
For best results, use high-resolution photos (minimum 1080×1080 px). We accept JPG, PNG, and HEIC formats.

💳 Payments & Returns

What payment methods are accepted?▼
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit Cards, Credit Cards, and Net Banking.
Can I return a custom product?▼
Since all products are custom-made, returns are accepted only if there is a quality defect or printing error on our end.
What coupon codes are available?▼
Try WELCOME10 for 10% off, FREESHIP for free delivery, or SAVE50 for ₹50 flat off. Coupons apply at checkout.
